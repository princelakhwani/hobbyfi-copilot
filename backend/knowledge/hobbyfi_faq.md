# HobbyFi Vendor Knowledge Base

## Membership

Membership can be extended by vendors.

Membership updates require vendor approval before execution.

## Trial

Trial users receive limited access.

Trial duration depends on sport.

## Revenue

Revenue is calculated from completed bookings.

## Attendance

Attendance is updated after each booking.

## Refund Policy

Refunds are approved only by the vendor.

## Cancellation

Bookings cancelled before 24 hours receive full refund.

Otherwise refund depends on vendor policy.

## Support

Vendor can contact HobbyFi support.
